The People Behind the Brew

Named After Her.
Built for Her.

"She didn't know it yet — but she already had a brand."

This is us — a small family from the highlands and lowlands of northern Luzon, with one big dream and one tiny reason to chase it.

He's a licensed pharmacist and a coffee enthusiast who takes his beans as seriously as his prescriptions. She's a native of Kalinga Province, where coffee has been part of the culture long before it became a trend. She didn't just grow up near the farms — she grew up knowing them.

And then there's AJ — their daughter, the smallest of the three, and honestly the whole point of this. The brand carries her initials. The shop is named after her cousin Zoie, who gave her the nickname "AJ" before she could even say it herself.

No grand mission statement. Just a family that loves good coffee, knows where it comes from, and figured — why not share it?
