Traditional Brewing

How to Brew
the Right Way.

01
Boil the Water
Bring fresh, clean water to a full boil. Use filtered water for the best taste.
02
Add Coffee
Add 1–2 tablespoons of ground Barako per cup. Adjust to your preferred strength.
03
Boil Together
Boil the coffee in the water for 2–3 minutes. This is what brings out the strong, bold flavor.
04
Strain & Serve
Pour through a strainer before drinking. Enjoy hot and savor the full aroma.
Important Note

This is not instant coffee. AJ's Premium Pure Barako is a traditional ground coffee that requires boiling and straining. The extra step is worth it — you'll taste the difference immediately.

Paraang ng Pagtimpla: Magpakulo ng tubig → Maglagay ng 1–2 kutsara kada tasa → Pakuluan ng 2–3 minuto → Salain bago inumin.
